J-Hope

Dance Leader, Rapper, Producer

About

Name: Jung Hoseok

Date of Birth: Feb 18, 1994

Hometown: Gwangju, South Korea

Height: 5'10"

Favorite Book: 20,000 Leagues Under the Sea

Hobbies: Dancing, Shopping, Photography

Behind the Music

J-Hope, fondly know as the sunshine member, made his debut with BTS June 13, 2013. He took on the role of dance leader in stride, responsible for making sure each member of the group knew to chereography as well as working with on and off site chereographers. It is not uncommon to find the younger members of BTS following behind him wherever he may go. With the support of his bandmates, J-hope released a solo mixtape called, Hope World, in 2018.

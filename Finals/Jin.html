About

Behind the Music

Jin made his debut with BTS, June 12, 2013. As the oldest member of the group, he is responsible for the groups well-being and based off korean hierarchy has the most authority. Besides his beautiful vocals, Jin is known as the visual of the group. He has coined the term World Wide Handsome, and seeks every oppurtunity to make those around him laugh using that term. He has released solo tracks,'Moon','Epiphany', and 'Awake'.

Behind the Music

Jungkook debuted with BTS June 12, 2013. He is the youngest member of the group and was given the name, 'The Golden Maknae', due to his ability to be able to do everything well. Due to his stable and beautiful vocals, he is the main vocalist of the group and you can often here his backing vocals in their music.He believes that he is a culmination of all the members put together. He released solo song, 'My Time' and 'Begin' and 'Euphoria'.

Behind the Music

Kim Namjoon joined the music scene at the age of 13 years old. He was introduced to the underground rap scene of south korea by his peers where he rose to success. Being the leader of BTS, he motivates, guides, and helps members of his team as well as settling any conflict between group members or within their production company. RM is heavily involved with both the writing of production of each song released. RM has released his solo album, 'Mono'.

About

Name: Park Jimin

Hometown: Busan, South Korea

Height: 5'9"

Favorite Book: Legendary Moonlight Sculptor

Hobbies: Dancing, Martial Arts,Travelling

Behind the Music

The hyung(oldest) of the maknae(young members) line, Jimin though the last member to join, made his debut with BTS June 12, 2013. Jimin, a counter-tenor is well known for his unique voice. He graduated top of his class from the Korean Arts Highschool, moving on to take a permanent place on the world stage. Jimin is responsible for excuting the majority of the stunts done on stage as well as assisting his fellow bandmates with chereography. He has released olo songs,'Serendipity', and 'Filter'.

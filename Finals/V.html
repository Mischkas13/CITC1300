Behind the Music

V made his debut with BTS June 12, 2013 as a suprise member. He was not revealed to be a member until the day of the group first performance. Following in the steps of World Wide Handsome Jin, V consistenly over the past year has been named the most handsome man in the world. Outside of being a visual for the group, V has taken on the networking role of the group, making friends where the group goes. V is the only lyric baritone of the group, giving each song the group releases a soulful texture. He has released solo songs such as, 'Singularity','Stigma', and 'Sweet Night'.

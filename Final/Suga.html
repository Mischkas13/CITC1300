Behind the Music

Suga is a many of may names; Yoongi and Agust D being a few. Suga depsite being the most laid back member of BTS, plays a large role in both the production and writing of the majority of BTS songs. On average, he writes and produces 200 songs per year and his one of the main producers of their record label. Suga has released his solo albums, 'D-2' and Agust D.

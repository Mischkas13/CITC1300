Korean Greetings

Depending on the scenario there are several different Korean greetings that can be used. Here are a few that you can choose from. Don't forget to bow when you're greeting someone new.

Hello(Informal)      Annyeong      안녕

Hello(On Phone)      Yeoboseyo      여보세요

Good Morning      Joeun Achimieyo      좋은아침이에요

Nice to meet you      Mannaseo Bangapseumnida      만나서 반갑습니다

How are you?      Eotteoke Jinaeseyo?      어떻게 지내세요?

Please look after me    Jalbutag Deurimnida      잘부탁드립니다
